Contrast Suggest

Accessibility contrast ratio checker: insert background and text color to the fields. The calculated contrast ratio will appear. If the contrast ratio is bellow 4.5, a suggested darker/lighter text color will appear.

The idea and the design are based on Lea Verou's excellent contrast ratio checker
